luna's portfolio

In October of 2024, the cat+fish team decided to make a free mini-magazine to generate publicity.
That idea eventually evolved into cat+fish Canned, a recurring side project with a rotating team.

I, along with a few other members, was in charge of the first issue of Canned.
I was responsible for the cover, back cover, two spreads, and general visual editing.
