luna's portfolio

Since 2023, I've been the lead editor on my fancy art school's magazine, cat+fish.
cat+fish is produced entirely by students and printed in partnership with the School of Visual Arts.
I'm in charge of a team of 30, I conduct interviews, and I connect with industry professionals.

The second issue of the magazine was the first issue that I was the lead on.
Among other things, it featured an interview with Gail Anderson, SVA's design chair.
